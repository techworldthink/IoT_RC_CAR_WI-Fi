# IoT RC CAR  - WI-Fi

### Componants
#### L298 Motor Driver x 2
#### Gear motor        x 4
#### ESP32 DevKit      x 1
#### 9 volt cell       x 2


### Connections

#### L298 - 1 connection
##### Motor 1  - connect front right motor
##### Motor 2  - connect front left motor
##### +ve - 9 +ve
##### -ve - 9 -ve
##### M11  - Pin 12
##### M12  - Pin 13
##### M21  - Pin 14
##### M22  - Pin 15
##### GND  - GND
##### EN-A - 3.3 V
##### EN-B - 3.3 V

#### L298 - 2 connection
##### Motor 1  - connect back right motor
##### Motor 2  - connect back left motor
##### +ve - 9 +ve
##### -ve - 9 -ve
##### M11  - Pin 5
##### M12  - Pin 18
##### M21  - Pin 19
##### M22  - Pin 21
##### GND  - GND
##### EN-A - 3.3 V
##### EN-B - 3.3 V
